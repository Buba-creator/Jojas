Our Services

Jolas Homes and Properties

We provide a multiplicity of real estate-related services to our clients. From property acquisition, development, to its valuation for resale, Jolas Homes and Properties is your best choice. Herein is a description of the services our clients enjoy:

Property and facility management

We help with and smoothen the procurement, leasing, acquisition, and disposal of landed properties. This purview of this management includes rent collection, repairs, preparation of agreements, ejection of defaulting tenants, and general maintenance of both residential and commercial properties.

Property development

You need a funding proposal drawn up? A feasibility report? Research on ROI, cost? Seeing a property from virgin land to ready-to-use property? Need help with planning and design? We have got you covered. We implore you to take full advantage of our experienced staff, partners, and contacts in the real estate industry.

Property valuation

Determining the worth of a property is both an art and a science and should be put in the hands of professionals, like us. At Jolas Homes and Properties, irrespective of reason for valuation –mortgage, property disposal, acquisition, rent rates, insurance-, we would give you an accurate worth of your landed assets at an affordable price.

Advisory services

Our advisory services are geared towards helping our clients make informed decisions. It includes asset review, feasibility modelling, financial modelling, risk management, and cost-benefit analysis.
